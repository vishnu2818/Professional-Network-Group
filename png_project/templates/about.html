About - Professional Network Group (PNG)

Professional Network Group (PNG) was founded by Er. Raja B.E., Managing Partner of RPR Group of Companies in October 2017 under the name D-Connect, a referral-based network designed to connect entrepreneurs and businesses. From October 2017 to August 2024, D-Connect facilitated business referrals worth ₹5 Crores across multiple industries, despite not having a formalized member base.

In oct’ 2024, D-Connect transitioned into a more structured and dynamic platform, rebranding as Professional Network Group (PNG). This transformation introduced premium membership opportunities, providing enhanced networking, learning, and collaboration experiences for our growing community.

Today, PNG continues to foster a vibrant environment where entrepreneurs, professionals, and business leaders unite to grow, collaborate, and succeed.
