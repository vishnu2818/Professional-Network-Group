Our Mission

PNG is a dynamic platform committed to connecting entrepreneurs and professionals through regular meetups centered around three core pillars: Developing Skills, Knowledge Sharing, and Value Creation (DKV). Our mission is to empower business owners and professionals to achieve sustainable growth, create new opportunities, and cultivate a culture of mutual success.

Developing Skills (D):
  • Workshops & Training: Providing a platform to enhance business and leadership skills in areas such as marketing, finance, leadership, and technology.
  • Peer-to-Peer Learning: Promoting mutual learning through networking and collaboration.
Knowledge Sharing (K):
  • Expert Talks & Panel Discussions: Facilitating a space for exchanging insights, experiences, and expertise.
  • Mentorship Programs: Connecting seasoned leaders with those eager to grow their knowledge and leadership skills.
Value Creation (V):
  • Collaborations & Partnerships: Encouraging entrepreneurs to create and exchange value through joint ventures, partnerships, and business referrals.
  • Business Opportunities: Building a network that supports collaboration and mutual growth.
Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading global platform for entrepreneurs and professionals, where ideas are nurtured, knowledge is shared, and businesses thrive through collaboration. We aim to create an inclusive ecosystem that empowers individuals and organizations to achieve sustainable growth, unlock new opportunities, and form long-lasting partnerships. Through skill development, innovation, and mutual value creation, PNG will serve as the catalyst for transforming the business landscape, enabling every member to achieve success and make a positive impact in their industries and communities. We are committed to continuous evolution, unwavering core values, and excellence.
